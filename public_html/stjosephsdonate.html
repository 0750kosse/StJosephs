The Parish Needs Your Support!  Please help by giving the benefit of Gift Aid. If you are a taxpayer the Government will add 25p for every pound that you donate.

The Gift Aid Scheme

St Joseph’s Parish is a registered charity. Under the Government’s Gift Aid scheme, for every £1 given by someone who has completed a simple Gift Aid Declaration, we can reclaim 25p from the tax man. The only condition for becoming a Gift Aid donor is that you are a taxpayer and you pay at least an equivalent amount of UK Income Tax and /or Capital Gains Tax (the Gift Aid Declaration states these conditions in full).

Please complete the donation form and if applicable the Gift Aid Declaration and return it to the Parish Office, indicating whether you wish to give your regular offertory donations by standing order or by the envelope system. If you wish to donate by standing order, please also complete the Standing Order Form and return it to the Parish Office or a member of the parish team.

Many thanks for your support.
