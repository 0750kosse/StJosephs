Adult Choir

The church choir consists of a small but dedicated group of singers who sing at 10 am and 12 noon Masses as well as for the major feasts of the church year. The choir has been in existence for many years and we see their role as enhancing the liturgy and supporting the singing of the congregation at the two Masses.

We are always keen to recruit new people and would welcome singers who are able to commit to attending rehearsals on a regular basis.  Please contact the Parish Office or one of the Parish Team if you are interested.

Children´s Liturgy

Lead by an experienced team the children’s liturgy takes place at 10am Mass and covers the Gospel of the day at a level the children can understand and enjoy. We enrich this with discussion, prayers, and activities. We aim to raise awareness of God’s love, the humanity of Jesus, the Seasons of the Church, the understanding of symbols in the Mass and the context of the Bible.

Bereavement Counselling

Counselling Is available at St Joseph’s.  Contact Elisabeth Eidinow via the Parish Office.

Prayer Groups

There are several prayer groups who meet on Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday.  For more information please contact the Parish Office.

Parish Social Events

We hold regular coffee mornings usually on the first Sunday of the month after 10 am Mass. We also have a summer party and Christmas bazaar.  These are marvellous ways to meet fellow parishioners and, in the case of the Christmas bazaar, to raise funds for church expenses.
